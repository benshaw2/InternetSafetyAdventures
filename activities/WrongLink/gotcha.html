Gotcha — this was a simulation

You clicked a link that did not take you to the movie. That can happen on real sites too — sometimes by mistake, or because someone is trying to trick you.

What happened?

  • The "Watch" button was programmed to redirect to this page so we could show how it feels when a link doesn't do what you expect.
  • Notice whether the link asked for anything (it didn’t) — never enter passwords or personal info on pages you weren’t expecting.

Tips to stay safe

  1. Always check the website name — are you on the site you expect?
  2. If a page looks strange or asks for extra info, ask a grown-up before continuing.
  3. Hover over links (or long-press on mobile) to see where they go — if unsure, don’t click.
  4. Use parental controls and talk openly about what sites are allowed.
