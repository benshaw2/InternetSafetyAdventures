🧠 What We Learned

In this activity, you played a fun game of “Online Trick-or-Treat.” The game asked you for more and more personal information — like your name, birthday, or even your address — to “win” candy.

When you entered your address, the game said your candy was on its way — but then you found out it wasn’t safe to share that kind of information!

🚫 Think Before You Share

  • Never share your real name, address, or birthdate online without checking with an adult.
  • Even if a game or website looks fun or friendly, it might not be safe to give out personal details.
  • Always ask a parent, teacher, or trusted adult before filling in forms online.

💡 Fun Fact

Websites sometimes ask for information that’s used to guess passwords or find where you live — that’s why we keep it private!
